Potomac Glen School Redistricting Task Force

Who We Are

The Potomac Glen School Redistricting Task Force is a volunteer-led group working to represent and protect the interests of our community during the MCPS boundary review process. Our mission is to ensure transparent, equitable, and community-focused solutions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is redistricting happening?
A: MCPS is opening a new high school and reviewing boundaries to manage capacity and demographics.

Q: How does this affect Potomac Glen?
A: Some proposals would move our students to different schools, breaking neighborhood continuity.

Our Findings & Analysis

We are analyzing the impact of each MCPS redistricting proposal on our community using demographic, distance, and capacity data.
